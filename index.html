About Achilles

At that time, I also installed a custom ROM (Operating System) and recovery on my Samsung Galaxy S3 - CyanogenMod and ClockWorkMod.

In high school, I built my first desktop computer and also set up and installed all of the necessary software on it. I also became a Windows Insider to test Windows 10 beginning with the Technical Preview. I also installed a custom ROM and recovery on my second phone, also an Android phone, which I still use today.

As I college student, I set up and installed a custom head unit (stereo) in my car that runs Android, allowing me to use any app on the Play Store and Android Auto through an emulator as well.
